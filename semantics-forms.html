Synopsis

Pink. Puffy. Powerful.

star star star

Learn the not-so-secret history of Nintendo's powerful pink puff, Kirby!

Don’t let the adorable face fool you—this powerful, pink puff can pack a punch! Since 1992, Kirby has been battling baddies across dozens of games. With his unique abilities, Kirby is always finding new ways to take on troublemakers.

Characters

Meet the stars of the Kirby game series.

face

Kirby

He may only be eight inches tall, but you should never underestimate this little pink hero. On the planet Pop Star, Kirby used his elastic body and his uncanny ability to absorb enemies to save Dream Land from the mischievous King Dedede. Since then, he's gone on countless other adventures and even become a force to be reckoned with in the Super Smash Bros.™ fighting game series.

Waddle Dee

Waddle Dees are the most common inhabitants of Dream Land. While mostly harmless, some Waddle Dees have been known to loyally serve King Dedede.

King Dedede

The greedy, self-proclaimed king of Dream Land, King Dedede is Kirby’s “arch-frenemy”. Armed with a giant hammer, King Dedede often causes trouble, but sometimes helps Kirby out in a pinch.

Meta Knight

The mysterious swordsman is rarely seen without his iconic mask—and when he does lose it in battle, he runs away! He likes to test his sword techniques out on Kirby, but sometimes serves as an ally.
